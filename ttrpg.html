TTRPGs.

My work-in-progress rulesystem is currently called Advanced Swords & Sorcery, but I need a better name. I like the X- & X- format common to the genre: Dungeons & Dragons, Tunnels & Trolls, Castles & Crusades, etc. The "Advanced" just comes from the covers of first edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons - it's taken wholesale from there, and actually isn't quite appropriate. One of my design goals is to streamline and minimize complexity, so it really isn't "Advanced." So if you have an idea for a better name, let me know.

Download the current version of the rules booklet. You can also access a draft of some optional rules, and a draft of best practices and tips for running the game. These drafts require citations which I have yet to give; reader be warned. Feedback and suggestions for all the documents are welcome!

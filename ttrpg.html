TTRPGs

My work in progress rulesystem is currently called Advanced Swords and Sorcery, but I need a better name. I like the X- & X- format: Dungeons & Dragons, Tunnels & Trolls, Castles & Crusades, etc. The "Advanced" just comes from the covers of first edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons - it's taken wholesale from there, and actually isn't quite appropriate. One of my design goals is to streamline and minimize complexity, so it really isn't "Advanced." So if you have an idea for a better name, let me know.
